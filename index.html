About Me

I am deeply passionate about cybersecurity and thrilled to explore its vast realm. Armed with a BCA degree and a fervor for penetration testing, I'm immersing myself in OWASP Top 10 and web application pentesting. Join me as I navigate the dynamic landscape of cybersecurity, blending expertise with an insatiable curiosity.
